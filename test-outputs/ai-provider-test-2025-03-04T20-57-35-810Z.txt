6. Try before you buy: If possible, try on shoes in a store to ensure they fit properly. You can also order from online retailers with good return policies.

Remember, the right shoe should feel comfortable right away and not require a break-in period. Happy shoe shopping!

Response: Taking care of your Nike shoes can help prolong their lifespan and keep them looking great. Here are some tips:

1. Regular Cleaning: Remove dirt and dust after each wear. You can use a soft cloth or a gentle brush.
